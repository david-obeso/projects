Discography

1st Single Album: IGNITION

Released: November 2025

The Concept: The "Big Bang" of the SOLØRIS universe—the moment the members realize their power to manipulate light and sound.

  • Track 01: Event Horizon (Title Track) A high-octane track featuring heavy cinematic synths and a "glitchy" bassline. The song describes the point of no return when falling in love—or falling into the SOLΛRIS orbit.
  • Track 02: Zero Gravity A dreamy, ambient R&B track that highlights the group’s vocal harmonies. It captures the feeling of weightlessness and escaping the pressures of the world.
  • Track 03: Event Horizon (Instrumental)

    • Music Video: "Event Horizon"

    The MV is a visual feast of "maximalist futurism." It opens with the four members in a monochromatic, desolate world that begins to "glitch" as they start to dance. As the chorus hits, the world explodes into vibrant, neon colors. Iconic scenes include Mina standing at the edge of a literal black hole and Jia’s solo dance break on a platform made of liquid mercury. The video ends with a cryptic shot of a dying star, setting the stage for their next chapter.

    1st Mini Album: REIGNITE THE STARS

    Released: February 2026

    The Concept: After the "Ignition," the members travel through different "phases of light" to find the source of the universe’s fading energy.

  • Track 01: Supernova Glitch (Title Track) An experimental pop track that "breaks" its own rhythm. It features unexpected genre shifts—from a haunting, operatic intro by Sora to a gritty, industrial hip-hop verse by Rumi.
  • Track 02: Prism An upbeat, synth-wave anthem about the beauty of diversity. "One light, seven colors," the lyrics suggest, celebrating the different personalities within the group.
  • Track 03: Dark Matter A dark, moody track with a heavy focus on the "Ethereal" side of their sound. It's slow-tempo but intense, utilizing whispered vocals and deep, resonating percussion.
  • Track 04: Solar Flare A high-energy "performance-heavy" track designed for dance challenges. It's the group's most accessible "pop" song, featuring a catchy brass hook.
  • Track 05: Solstice (Fan Song) A warm, acoustic-led ballad dedicated to their fandom, Solstice. It's a promise that no matter how dark the dimension gets, they will always find their way back to their fans.

    • Music Video: "Supernova Glitch"

    Filmed with cutting-edge AR (Augmented Reality) technology, this MV is designed to feel like an interactive video game. The members are "glitching" between two worlds: a high-fashion digital "void" and a lush, alien forest under a purple sun. The choreography is the highlight here, featuring "stutter" movements that mimic a digital lag. The MV ends with the members successfully "reigniting" a giant star, which turns out to be a massive heart made of light—the "Core" of their universe.
