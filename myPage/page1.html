Members

Mina (Leader / Lead Vocal)

As the undisputed anchor of SOLØRIS, Mina brings a calm, strategic authority that perfectly balances the group’s explosive energy. Known for her razor-sharp precision and "cool-toned" charisma, she is the primary architect behind the group's "Visionary" concept. Before debuting, Mina was celebrated in the underground scene for her unique songwriting, and she continues to have a strong hand in shaping the direction of SOLØRIS’s sound, ensuring their music always aligns with their cerebral lore.

Within the SOLΛRIS universe, Mina represents the "Solar Minimum"—the period of calm before a great celestial shift. Her vocals are characterized by their smooth, crystalline texture, guiding listeners through the opening verses of their songs like a navigator navigating the dark. While other members provide the heat, Mina provides the essential direction, making her an indispensable leader both on and off the stage.

Sora (Main Vocal)

Sora is the literal "voice of the sun." Possessing a rare, three-and-a-half-octave range and a tone that is simultaneously delicate and devastatingly powerful, she is the emotive heart of SOLΛRIS. Sora is tasked with delivering the climatic high notes and soaring choruses that define "Ethereal-Pop." Her ability to transition instantly from an airy, haunting whisper to a full-belt powerhouse is what gives the group's music its signature dynamic tension.

Representing the "Supernova," Sora's energy is explosive and captivating. During the pre-debut "Lunar Sessions," her live cover of a complex ballad went viral instantly, cementing her reputation as one of the genre's new premier vocal talents. She is the member who brings the heat and intensity, cutting through the complex, bass-heavy synth tracks with undeniable clarity and emotion.

Jia (Main Dancer / Sub-Vocal)

Jia is the physical embodiment of the group's "glitch" aesthetic. Trained in both classical ballet and avant-garde street styles, her dancing style is described as "experimental" and almost superhumanly precise. She is responsible for executing the group's most complex formations and center-pieces. Jia doesn't just dance; she interprets the digital and celestial concepts into sharp, flowing movements that make her magnetic to watch.

In the SOLΛRIS narrative, Jia embodies the "Aurora"—the visual manifestation of energy interacting with the atmosphere. Her stage presence is often described as enigmatic, drawing eyes with her sleek visual and slightly distant demeanor until the beat drops. While her primary focus is dance, her breathy, unique vocal tone is used strategically in tracks to add layers of texture to the "Ethereal" soundscape.

Rumi (Main Rapper / Lead Dancer / Maknae)

Rumi may be the group's youngest member (maknae), but she commands the stage with the fierce confidence of a seasoned pro. Her rapping is sharp-tongued, rhythmic, and intricate, providing a necessary grit and contrast to Sora's soaring vocals. Known for her rapid-fire flow and playful, confident stage persona, Rumi is the "edge" of SOLΛRIS, often delivering the defining "girl crush" lines in their music.

Rumi represents the "Eclipse"—the unexpected shadow that makes the light seem even brighter. Her energy is spontaneous and witty, serving as the playful counterweight to Mina's serious leadership. Despite her fierce rap persona, she is beloved by fans for her unexpected, clumsy charm in variety content, proving she is truly a multifaceted artist who isn't afraid to step outside of the mold.
