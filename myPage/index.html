About SOLØRIS

Born from the intersection of high-fashion elegance and futuristic "glitch" aesthetics, SOLØRIS is a four-member powerhouse redefining the boundaries of the "girl crush" genre. The group's name represents their role as a central sun in the music industry—a source of heat, light, and gravity that pulls listeners into their unique cinematic universe. Rather than sticking to one sound, SOLØRIS focuses on "Ethereal-Pop," a blend of heavy synth-bass, haunting melodies, and intricate rap verses that feel both alien and familiar.

The lineup consists of four distinct "elements" that complete the solar system: Mina (the visionary leader), Sora (the powerhouse main vocal), Jia (the experimental main dancer), and Rumi (the sharp-tongued maknae). Each member represents a different phase of light, from the blinding "Supernova" energy of their stage performances to the "Eclipse" mystery of their conceptual storytelling. Together, they aim to prove that strength isn't just about power—it's about the brilliance you leave behind.

Since their viral pre-debut "Lunar Sessions," SOLØRIS has built a reputation for high-concept storytelling. Their narrative follows four travelers from a fading dimension who must use the power of sound to reignite the stars. This "lore" is woven into every music video and stage outfit, utilizing reflective fabrics, holographic visuals, and avant-garde choreography that mimics the movement of celestial bodies.

They aren't just a girl group; they are a multi-sensory experience designed to break the mold. With their debut single, "Event Horizon," SOLØRIS invites fans (known affectionately as Solstice) to step out of the shadows and into the light. The group champions themes of self-actualization and defying expectations, encouraging their audience to find their own "inner sun." As they prepare to take the global stage, one thing is certain: the world is about to get a lot brighter.
